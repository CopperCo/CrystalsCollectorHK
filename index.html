Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are 4 crystals shown below. By clicking you will add a specific amount of points to your total scored.

You win the game by mathcing your total score to the random number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will change.
